# 🚴 Bike Sales Intelligence Dashboard

## 📊 Project Overview

This project presents a **Sales Analysis Dashboard** built using **Power BI** to analyze the performance of a bicycle retail business.

The goal of this project is to extract meaningful insights from sales data to support **business decision-making**, identify trends, evaluate store performance, and understand customer distribution.

The dashboard provides a clear and interactive way to explore sales performance across **products, stores, time, and customer locations**.

---

# 📁 Dataset

The dataset represents a bicycle retail company and includes multiple tables such as:

* Orders
* Order Items
* Customers
* Stores
* Products
* Categories
* Brands
* Staff
* Stocks

The data covers the period from **2016 to 2018**.

---

# ❓ Business Questions

This analysis aims to answer several key business questions:

1. What is the total revenue generated by the company?
2. Which store generates the highest sales?
3. Which products contribute the most to revenue?
4. Which product categories perform best?
5. How do sales change over time?
6. Is there seasonality in sales?
7. Where are most customers located?
8. What is the distribution of order statuses?

---

The dashboard includes:

# 🔍 Key Insights

### 1️⃣ Store Performance

Baldwin Bikes is the top-performing store with approximately **$1.72M in revenue**, representing the majority of total sales.

Rowlett Bikes shows the lowest performance with around **$0.27M**, indicating potential operational or market challenges.

---

### 2️⃣ Product Performance

The top-selling products are dominated by **Trek bicycles**, with:

* Trek Fuel EX 8 29 generating about **$158K** in revenue.
* Trek Slash 8 27.5 generating about **$157K**.

Mountain bikes appear to be the most successful category overall.

---

### 3️⃣ Sales Trends

Sales show a clear **seasonal pattern**, with **April being the strongest month**.

After May, sales gradually decline throughout the rest of the year.

---

### 4️⃣ Customer Distribution

Approximately **70% of customers come from New York (NY)**.

This indicates a strong market presence in NY but also highlights a **risk of over-reliance on a single region**.

---

### 5️⃣ Order Status

Most orders fall under **Order Status 4**, representing the majority of completed orders.

This suggests efficient order processing and fulfillment.

---

# 💡 Business Recommendations

Based on the insights, several actions could improve business performance:

### Expand Market Reach

Reduce reliance on New York by increasing marketing and sales efforts in other states such as **California and Texas**.

### Improve Underperforming Stores

Investigate why **Rowlett Bikes** is underperforming and consider improvements in marketing, inventory management, or staffing.

### Focus on Best-Selling Products

Increase promotion and inventory for high-performing products such as **Trek mountain bikes**.

### Leverage Seasonal Demand

Run targeted promotions before peak months such as **April** to maximize revenue.

### Boost End-of-Year Sales

Introduce promotions during **Black Friday and holiday seasons** to counter the drop in sales during the second half of the year.
